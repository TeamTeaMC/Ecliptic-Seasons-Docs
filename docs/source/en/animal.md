> All the following features require the corresponding configuration to be enabled.

## Animal Breeding Seasons

Animal breeding is now restricted by seasons. Note that this behavior is also related to the agricultural climate zone.

## Bees

Bees have special seasonal behavior: they will only collect nectar during spring, and they will not leave their hives in
winter.

## Fishing

Fishing is also affected by seasonal constraints. Outside of summer or during thunderstorms, the fishing success rate
will be reduced.
